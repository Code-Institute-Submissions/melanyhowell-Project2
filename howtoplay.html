How To Play

  • To play you need to find two matching pairs for each set of cards on the screen
  • To reveal a car click on it then go onto another card and click on that, if they match well done
  • If they don't unlucky, but try to remeber their locations incase they match the next cards you turnover
  • There is a timer and moves counter so you can see if you can beat your best scores
  • Good Luck and get clicking!
